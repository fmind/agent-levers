# §Plan section template

Appended below the last existing LEVER.md section by the plan step. Empty sections: write a single `- none` bullet rather than omit the section. No trailing status-formatted line.

```markdown
## Plan

### Goal

<one sentence>

### Investigation findings

#### Codebase

- <fact>
  source: `<file:line>` / `<commit>`

#### External

- <fact or short digest>
  source: `<url>` (retrieved <YYYY-MM-DD>)

#### Resolved questions

- <Q from brief> → <A>; source: `<file:line>` / `<url>`

### Constraints

- <invariant do must not violate>

### Risks & mitigations

- <bullet>

### Deferred

- <bullet>
```
